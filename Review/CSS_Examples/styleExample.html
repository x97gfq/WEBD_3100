Nova Scotia Community College

Burridge Campus

A welcoming campus with big school spirit, Burridge operates from two locations along one of the province’s most beautiful shores – the main site in Yarmouth and the Digby Learning Centre.

Take advantage of the services available through the campus spa, nail and hair salon, where students apply what they’ve learned. On Wednesdays, you’ll want to head to the cafeteria to try the campus’s famous cinnamon buns.
